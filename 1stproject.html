Introduction
According to Philstar (GLOBAL) August 29, 2019, 3.3 million Filipinos suffer from depression which is one of the highest rates in Southeast Asia 2020. As a result, a lot of people are now engaging into planting according to the Article entitled “New Plant Lovers Emerge from Quarantine” to relieve the stress while staying at home which is proven according to the expert in the article titled “The Prescription for a Healthy Life? A House Plant! Indoor Greenery Boosts Mood, Reduces Stress and Cleans the Air around Us”, and now they are called the "plantito" and "plantita" who love to take care of plants.
My Role
I'm the lead designer and programmer for this Capstone project. I collaborated with project member (Dixter Baltazar, Aldrin Rulona, Rustom Tiamson, Josie Luaya, Jeraldine Delicano).
Problem
During the strike of pandemic, community quarantine has been the way of the government to prevent wide spread of the Covid-19 virus throughout the whole country. While being stucked in the house lots of our countrymen are on their effort to cure their boredom and most of all the most common problem in every household which is financial problem. Lots of people engaged themselves in indoor planting as the way of fighting stress but as they start we may say that they might get stressed if the plant grows unhealthy.
Specifif Problem
• Limited knowledge about indoor plants.
• Difficulty in monitoring the live status of indoor plants.
• Manual checking of soil status.
• Unnecessary amount of water used in watering the indoor plant.
• Manual watering of indoor plant.
• No temperature checks in the surroundings of plants.
Goal
This study aims to develop “Indoor plants watering support and maintenance system” that could help the user to monitor the live status of their plants via mobile app.
Specifif Goal
• To provide knowledge about indoor plants.
• To develop a system that would help in easy monitoring of indoor plants.
• To automated soil humidity monitoring.
• To help save unnecessary amount of water being used in watering indoor plant.
• To provide automated indoor plant watering support system.• To provide temperature checks and avoid reaching critical temperature that could harm indoor plant.
Targe User
To the plantitos/plantitas Help them in taking care of their plants. The system has an archive of main information about the indoor plants that would help them to understand more about the plants and which benefit would a specific plant be able to contribute in their environment
