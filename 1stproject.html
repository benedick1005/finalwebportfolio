Introduction

Year (2020) people worldwide got affected by the pandemic and the philippineswas among the them. Because of that our country was placed under community quarantine to help the government avoid the widespread of the virus through the entire nation. Many people stayed at home for their safety and to get away from the covid-19 virus infection. According to Philstar (GLOBAL) August 29, 2019, 3.3 million Filipinos suffer from depression which is one of the highest in southeast Asia. As a result , a lot of people are now engaging into planting according to the article entitled "New Plant Lovers Emerge from Quarantine" to relieve the stress while staying at home which is proven according to the expert in the article titled" The Prescription for a Healthy Life" A house Plant ! Greenery Boots Mood, Reduces Stress and Clean the Air around Us", and now they are call the "Plantito " and "Plantita " who love to take care of plants
